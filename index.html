Citizen Action of New York Organizing Calculator

Instructions: This tool helps you calculate how many people you need to contact to achieve your organizing goals. Enter your required number of contacts and the contact method, and the tool will show you the estimated number of outreach actions (e.g., phonebanks, canvassing shifts) required, considering a "flake factor" for no-shows.

Steps:

  1. Enter how many people you need to contact.
  2. Select your preferred contact method (e.g., phonebanking or canvassing).
  3. Input the expected contact rate (percentage of people reached).
  4. Set the "flake factor" (percentage of people who will not show up).
  5. Click "Calculate" to generate your outreach plan.

Contact Rates for Each Outreach Method:

  • Phonebanking (VPB): 8%
  • Canvassing: 15%
  • Tabling: 25%
  • Street Canvassing: 10%
